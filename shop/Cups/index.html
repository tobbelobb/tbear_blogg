These cups are made to be pleasant to drink from. Their shape is generated with a carefully crafted computer program. Each cup is 3D printed, hand carved and double-glazed individually. Visible 3D printed texture on the outside, smooth inside.

  • Size: Mid-size coffee cup
  • Clay type: Porcelain
  • Glazing technique: Dipped in white or transparent and sprayed with blue or green tones
  • Digital shaping: Custom hand-coded script
  • Manual shaping: Fettling knife on top, bottom, inside
  • Price: 150 EUR.
  • Second sorting items: 75 EUR.
