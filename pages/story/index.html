Hello!

Torbjørn Ludvigsen is an engineer, inventor and artist residing in Gothenburg, Sweden. He is known as the inventor of Hangprinter, which he started developing during his Master studies in physics 2014. He belives the society benefits from Rep Rap (länk?) and open source (länk?) technology as a way to speed up technical development and make it more available for common people. He has therefore published all his work under open licence(?) since the begining and built a community around it (länk). On his blog (länk) you can read about technical development issues and his thoughts in general around his work. Recently Hangprinter was covered in several newspapers (länkar) for winning a patent case, with crowd founded support.

He has also invented some lesser known features, like a visual position mesurement system originally ment to facilitate the calibration process of Hangprinter (länk) and line collision detector (länk).

In reacent years he has started using his own modern technology to create artwork that he loves. As a sailor growing up at the coast, he takes inspiration from the sea. "I want to make things that I want in my own home, and things that I want to give as a gift to my loved ones", he says.
